Moon Void of Course

The Moon is Void of Course when it makes no more major aspects (conjunction, sextile, square, trine, or opposition) to any planet before leaving its current zodiac sign.

It is believed that anything undertaken during such a period will bring no result.

On the positive side, if something risky or difficult is undertaken, something where other people can create a problem or interrupt you, this time seems to be beneficial, because those who might create a problem can do nothing against you.

The last aspect shows the mood, or the flavor, of the void-of-course period. Depending on it, the period can be more positive, or more stressful.
